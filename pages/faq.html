Legal & Documentation

What documents do I need for a property purchase?

▼

Essential documents include: Government ID, proof of income/funds, property deed, land survey report, tax clearance certificate, and encumbrance statement. Our team will provide a complete checklist tailored to your specific transaction.

How long does the legal documentation process take?

▼

The documentation and legal process typically takes 30-45 days from application to property registration. This may vary depending on government processing times and document complexity.

Do you handle legal compliance verification?

▼

Yes, we provide comprehensive legal verification including title deed checks, encumbrance reports, tax clearance verification, and zoning compliance confirmation for all properties.

Construction & Projects

What is the typical timeline for a construction project?

▼

Construction timelines depend on project size and complexity. A typical residential home takes 12-18 months, while larger commercial projects may take 18-36 months. We provide detailed timelines with progress tracking.

Do you provide construction warranties?

▼

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties covering structural integrity, construction quality, and materials for a specified period after completion. Warranty details are included in your contract.

Gems & Jewelry

How do you verify gem authenticity?

▼

All gemstones are verified by certified gemologists using international standards. We provide detailed certification reports and can arrange independent third-party verification if needed.

What guarantees do you offer with gem purchases?

▼

We guarantee authenticity, quality, and certification for all gemstone purchases. Money-back guarantees are available within 30 days if certification is not as represented.
